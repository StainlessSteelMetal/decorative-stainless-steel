💡 Why Choose Anti-Scratch Stainless Steel?

Compared with traditional smooth stainless steel surfaces, anti-scratch finishes offer:

✔ Reduced visible scratches
✔ Longer lifespan
✔ Lower maintenance costs
✔ Better visual consistency over time

Textured surfaces such as embossed or patterned sheets are especially effective in high-traffic environments.
